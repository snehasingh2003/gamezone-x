How to Play Dino Game

1. Controls: Use the up arrow or 'W' key to jump, and the right and left arrows or 'D' and 'A' keys to move.
2. Objective: Help the dinosaur survive by avoiding obstacles.
3. Scoring: Your score is displayed at the top right. Cover more distance for higher scores.
4. Difficulty: The game gets faster over time. React quickly to avoid obstacles.
5. Game Over: Colliding with an obstacle ends the game. Press reload to play again.
Enjoy playing the Dino Game!
